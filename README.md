## Hi there 👋 My Name is Christian
I am a Computer Science Junior at the University of Illinois Chicago with a passion for 2D Game Design and software logic.

# 📌My Journey
My interest in technology started in High School when I created a simple game about my dog Tank. A game where you eat fruit to gain points while there is a timer going off. Seeing that project come to life sparked my curiosity, leading me to Wilbur Wright College, where I earned my Associate's degree as a Star Scholar. That same persistence drives me today at UIC as I bridge the gap between academic theory and hands-on production.

# 👨‍💻 What I am Working On
Currently, I am teaching myself Unity and C# to pursue a career in Game Dev. I am also working on a Bachelor's degree and getting more involved in the CS community.

# 💻 Beyond Coding
When I am not behind my laptop, I am active in the Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers (SHPE) at UIC. Serving on the MemberSHPE committee has allowed me to sharpen my leadership and communication skills while helping to grow a supportive community for Hispanic STEM professionals.

I am currently seeking internship opportunities where I can apply my creativity, logic-based problem-solving, and work ethic to make a tangible impact in the software industry.
